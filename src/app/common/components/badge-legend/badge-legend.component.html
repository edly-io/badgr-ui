Legend

The badges are divided into four categories:

PROFESSIONAL COMPETENCE

A badge from the 'Professional Competence' category reflects a specific achievement in a technical area level. The level achieved can be classified into one of six consecutive levels become:

A1: Beginner
A2: Explorer
B1: Insider
B2: Expert
C1: Leader
C2: Pioneer

META COMPETENCE]

Badges from the 'Metacompetence' category represent higher-level skills from the areas of strategic tegical and problem-solving work. Here the service is also structured into six consecutive of the building stages:

A1: Beginner
A2: Explorer
B1: Insider
B2: Expert
C1: Leader
C2: Pioneer

PARTICIPATION

A badge from the 'Participation' category is independent of services provided and can awarded or obtained through attendance at listed events.

MEMBER

Membership in a club, institution or similar characterizes people for this to receive a badge from the 'Member' category. The service provided is not here significant.
